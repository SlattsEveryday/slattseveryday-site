Bio

Slatts Everyday is an actor and creative working across screen, performance, and music. His work spans short and feature films, documentary series, voice over, and collaborative creative projects.

He is drawn to character-driven material and grounded, naturalistic performances, with experience across narrative, documentary, and audio formats. Alongside acting, he maintains an active creative practice in music and live performance, which informs a versatile and instinctive approach to screen work.

This site presents selected work and current projects. Additional material is available on request.
